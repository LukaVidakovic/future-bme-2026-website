Wine House Kovacevic

S​ituated near Novi Sad, Wine House Kovačević in Irig is nestled within the Fruska Gora National Park, an area revered for its breathtaking scenery and as a cradle of natural and cultural heritage. This family-operated winery, with a legacy spanning over a century embodies both tradition and innovation. Rooted in traditional values, Kovačević Winery simultaneously holds an innovative vision, dedicated to crafting exceptional wines that harmonize time-honored practices with modern techniques. Through tastings that capture the unique flavors and characteristics of the region, Kovačević's commitment to producing high-quality Serbian wines is widely recognized both locally and on the global stage.

 Kralja Petra 221, Irig 22406, Serbia
