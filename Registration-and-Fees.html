Registration and Fees

Registration is mandatory for all participants, regardless of whether they will be attending the conference in person or not

  • Publication of one paper requires prior payment of one registration fee, regardless of the number of authors (is there one author or two/three co-authors).
  • Regular registration fee 180 ​€ (21.000 RSD) - Publication of one full paper up to 8 pages (A4) in the conference proceedings, one certificate of presentation or attendance in e-form for first co/author (will be delivered via email after the conference), refreshment breaks, gala dinner, certificate of participation, and additional activities for first co/author.
  • Co-authors who wish to attend the conference and/or receive a certificate, are obliged to pay the additional registration fee for Accompanying person 130​€ (15.210 RSD)
  • In case there are more than three coauthors, for each additional coauthor, 20 € will be added to the overall registration fee amount.
  • If a full paper exceeds 8 pages (A4 size), each additional page will be subject to a charge of 20 €.
